FAQ

How do I use the Crossword Solver?

1. Select word length from dropdown
2. Enter known letters in positions
3. Use anagram mode for scrambled letters

How do I use the Wordle Solver?

1. Enter your word
2. Tap letters to match Wordle colors
3. Get suggestions for next guess

How do I use Letter Boxed?

1. Enter the 12 letters clockwise
2. Find solutions or get hints
3. Use recent boxes feature for quick access
